My Vision

My Vision for the coding camps I offer is to provide quality education in a fun, challenging and nuturing environment.

Expectations

Work hard & play hard

My coding camps have a lot of interactive learning which include assignments, quizes, projects etc to keep the kids challenged. It is a student-teacher-parent commitment to master the subject. I am 100% committed and I expect the same from my kids & parents.
Remember to have realistic expectations and like learning anything else, it takes time, effort and there is no magic. Magic happens when you work hard & you can play hard. Also practice keeps your skills you learnt sharp. :) My class size is max 6 kids in a batch. I offer online and in-person classes.
